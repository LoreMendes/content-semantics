Beets Beetroot (Beta vulgaris) Common varieties Nutritional information Tags A bundle of organic beets from a local farm food co-op program. 55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the root. The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.
